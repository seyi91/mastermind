MASTERMIND

When you press PLAY, a secret random 4-digit number will be generated (all digits will be between 0 and 7). You have 10 attempts to guess the correct 4 digit combination!

Click the 'PLAY' button to start a game!

Upcoming Features!

  • High Score Tracker
    Game will keep track of your lowest number of attempts to guess the right solution as you play!
  • Login Functionality
    Player will be able to login and have access to results from previous games!
